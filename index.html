Bret Gold Issues 2v1 Challenge — "Beat Me and I'll Let You Quit"

In a stunning development, former 90s pro dooramper Bret Gold has issued a direct challenge to retirees Owen Buckwalter and Max M: face him in a 2v1 match, and if they win, he will formally accept their retirements. Gold, known for his dominant run in the early dooramp era, reportedly made the challenge public just days after Owen's retirement announcement. "You don't get to walk away like that," Gold is said to have stated. "Earn it." No match date has been confirmed.

May 27, 2026

Owen Buckwalter Retires — One Week Before Season Opener

In a blow to the NDL community, Owen Buckwalter has announced his retirement from competitive dooramp — just one week before the start of the regular season. The timing has left fans and fellow players stunned, with many calling it one of the most heartbreaking exits in recent NDL memory. Buckwalter offered no official statement on his reasoning. The league has yet to comment on how his roster spot will be handled heading into opening week.
