Coming into 2026 Temporary

The following rule changes take effect for the 2026 season. These updates reflect ongoing efforts to evolve competitive play.

3-Pointer Off the Dome — Ball Goes Live

If a player shoots a 3-pointer that hits the dome, the ball is considered live. Play continues — no dead ball, no reset.

Bounce-In Shots Count

Any shot that bounces before entering the basket is a valid made basket. This rule applies to all standard shot attempts during live play, including free throws. There is no goaltending on bounce-in shots.

Free Throw — Fadeaway Off the Back of the Tramp

Free throw attempts are no longer taken from a stationary position. The shooter must attempt a fadeaway shot and land off the back of the trampoline. Standard free throw point values apply.
