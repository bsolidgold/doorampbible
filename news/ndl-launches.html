The NDL is Here.

The National Dooramp League has officially launched its home on the internet. The Dooramp Bible — the official online hub of the NDL — is now live and open to players, fans, and anyone curious about the sport of dooramp.

This site is the definitive record of everything the NDL stands for: the rules, the history, the players, and the moments that have defined competitive dooramp. Whether you're a longtime player or someone who just picked up a doorampball for the first time, this is where you come to learn, track, and stay up to date.

What's Here Now

The site launches with a full rulebook, a growing history section covering the origins and evolution of the sport, and a player stats page with career records for current and former competitors. News coverage — including breaking stories, retirement announcements, and challenges — will be updated on an ongoing basis.

What's Coming

The NDL is just getting started online. In the pipeline: a searchable player database with full career breakdowns, an official shop for dooramp equipment, and live broadcasting coverage of the Winterdome — the league's annual championship tournament. More features will roll out as the season progresses.

The NDL has always been built on competition, history, and community. Now it has a home that reflects all of that. Welcome to the Dooramp Bible.
